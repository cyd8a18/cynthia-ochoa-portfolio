ISPIL: Interactive Sub-Goal-Planning Imitation Learning

PhD Research Project

Open video on YouTube

ISPIL is an interactive imitation learning framework developed during my PhD research to help robots complete long-horizon manipulation tasks with diverse goals. The method learns a hierarchical policy from human guidance, using sub-goal planning to break complex behaviors into manageable steps and reduce the amount of expert demonstration needed.
